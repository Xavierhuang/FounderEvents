# Privacy Policy for Founder Events

**Last Updated:** [Date]

## Introduction

Founder Events ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application ("App").

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide
- **Calendar Data:** We access your iOS Calendar to read and write events. This data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers unless you explicitly choose to sync or share events.
- **LinkedIn Profile Information:** If you choose to add LinkedIn profiles to events, this information is stored locally on your device.
- **Event Information:** Events you create or discover through the app are stored locally on your device.

### Information Automatically Collected
- **Device Information:** We may collect information about your device, including device model, operating system version, and unique device identifiers.
- **Usage Data:** We may collect information about how you use the App, such as which features you access and how often you use the App.

### Third-Party Services
- **OpenAI API:** When you use AI-powered features, event information may be sent to OpenAI for processing. Please review OpenAI's privacy policy: https://openai.com/policies/privacy-policy
- **Event Data APIs:** We fetch event information from third-party APIs. This data is used to display events in the App.
- **Google Calendar (if integrated):** If you choose to sync with Google Calendar, your calendar data will be processed according to Google's privacy policy.

## How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve the App
- Process and manage calendar events
- Provide AI-powered event extraction features
- Display event discovery information
- Enable route planning features
- Respond to your requests and provide customer support

## Data Storage and Security

- **Local Storage:** Most of your data is stored locally on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.
- **Security:** We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your information. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure.

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal information to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- **Service Providers:** We may share information with third-party service providers who perform services on our behalf (e.g., OpenAI for AI processing).
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose your information if required by law or in response to valid requests by public authorities.
- **Business Transfers:** In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred.

## Your Rights and Choices

You have the right to:
- **Access:** Request access to your personal information
- **Delete:** Delete your data by uninstalling the App or using in-app deletion features
- **Opt-Out:** Disable certain features that collect information (e.g., location services)
- **Data Portability:** Export your calendar data through standard iOS calendar export features

## Children's Privacy

Our App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.
