## üìù App Store Review Guidelines Compliance

### Key Guidelines to Follow:
1. **Functionality:** App must work as described
2. **Privacy:** Must have privacy policy and proper data declarations
3. **Content:** No offensive, illegal, or misleading content
4. **Metadata:** Accurate descriptions and keywords
5. **Design:** Professional appearance, no placeholder content
6. **Performance:** App must not crash or have major bugs

### Common Rejection Reasons:
- Missing privacy policy
- App crashes on launch
- Placeholder content
- Misleading descriptions
- Missing required permissions
- Broken functionality

## üöÄ Submission Steps

1. **Prepare All Materials**
   - Complete the checklist above
   - Create screenshots
   - Write descriptions
   - Set up privacy policy

2. **Archive Your App**
   - In Xcode: Product ‚Üí Archive
   - Wait for archive to complete

3. **Upload to App Store Connect**
   - In Xcode Organizer: Click "Distribute App"
   - Select "App Store Connect"
   - Follow the upload wizard

4. **Complete App Store Connect Listing**
   - Log into App Store Connect
   - Create new app (if first submission)
   - Fill in all required information
   - Upload screenshots
   - Add app description and metadata

5. **Submit for Review**
   - Complete all required fields
   - Add version information
   - Submit for review

6. **Wait for Review**
   - Typically 24-48 hours
   - You'll receive email notifications
   - Check App Store Connect for status
